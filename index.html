About Samli Thrift

My name is Ogun Lilian, the founder of Samli Thrift. I started this business to provide quality okirika at affordable prices. I personally inspect every bale and tell customers the true condition. What you see is what you get.

Here at Samli, we believe that everyone deserves to look good, feel good and still have some change in their pocket. Are you a student managing your funds, a worker upgrading your wardrobe or a retailer looking for stick to resell? Don't worry - we got you!

I personally inspect my goods and always tell customers the true condition — no fake grading, no hidden surprises. This honesty has helped me grow through referrals and returning customers.

Today, I supply quality thrift clothing to resellers and individuals across Nigeria. My goal is simple: sell good clothes, earn trust, and build long-term relationships.

Quality dey. Affordability dey. Na Samli Thrift be the plug.
